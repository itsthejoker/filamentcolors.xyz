FilamentColors

Have you ever wanted to see how two different manufacturer's filaments work together under the same light? Have you ever been tasked with figuring out what filaments to buy to get the perfect combination, then gotten something that's not quite what you were looking for?

Filamentcolors.xyz allows you to take a look at different 3D printer filaments in all sorts of shades from lots of different manufacturers, allowing you to get a better idea of what you want to buy.

IMPORTANT NOTE:

This site does not attempt to show you the "true color" of any given filament. The goal is to show you how colors of different filaments interact under the same light, as all manufacturers have their own samples photographed, but every manufacturer uses different lighting / camera setups (so comparing across manufacturers is really annoying, if not impossible).

Every swatch is photographed under the same (or as similar as possible) conditions, then analyzed for the average color across the swatch. That color is stored in hex color format, then used to help find colors (and used to help find similar colors / complement colors!). It also allows you to grab the hex color of the things you see here, then plug those values into any color picker site to help you figure out what colors work best for you.
