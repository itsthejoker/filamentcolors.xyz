Monetary Donations

FilamentColors.xyz is a labor of love for me, a passion project that I've dedicated countless hours to in order to help others bring their projects to life in the most vibrant and beautiful ways possible. From 3D printing enthusiasts to artists and designers, I've heard from so many people who have benefited from the site; are you one of them?

But as much as I love running this site and providing this resource it's no secret that it takes a lot of time, energy, and money to keep it going. From server costs and equipment updates to the never-ending quest for new and exciting filaments, there are always expenses that need to be covered in order to keep FilamentColors.xyz alive and thriving. I pay for everything required to run this site out of pocket. Monetary donations help us in:

  • finding more filament from across the world
  • equipment maintenance & upgrades
  • infrastructure maintenance & upgrades
  • attending events to talk about the site

If you've found value in FilamentColors.xyz, if it's helped you bring your projects to life in ways you never thought possible, or if you simply believe in the power of community and creativity, I would be incredibly grateful if you would consider making a donation to help keep this site alive. No amount is too small, and every little bit helps. Your contributions will go directly towards covering the costs of running and improving FilamentColors.xyz, allowing me to continue providing this valuable resource to all of you.

So if you're able, please consider making a donation today. Your support means the world to me, and it will help ensure that FilamentColors.xyz can continue to be a source of inspiration and creativity for years to come.
